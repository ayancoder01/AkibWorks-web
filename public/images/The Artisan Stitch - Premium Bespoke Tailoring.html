Our Process

The Way WeWork

Our time-tested process ensures every garment meets our exacting standards. From initial consultation to final delivery, we're with you every step of the way.

01

Book Appointment

Schedule a consultation at your convenience. We'll discuss your requirements, style preferences, and fabric choices.

  • Free consultation
  • Style guidance
  • Fabric selection
  • Timeline discussion
02

Pick & Measured

Our master tailors take precise measurements and help you choose the perfect fabric from our premium collection.

  • 30+ measurements
  • Posture analysis
  • Fabric consultation
  • Design finalization
03

Swatch & Stitch

Expert craftsmen begin the meticulous process of cutting and stitching your garment with attention to every detail.

  • Hand-cut patterns
  • Expert stitching
  • Quality checks
  • Progress updates

Trial & Delivery

  • Final fitting
